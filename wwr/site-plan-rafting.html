Overview

Purpose

the purpose of establishing an online presence for the rafting business is to reach a broader audience, increase bookings, build trust, and provide valuable information while fostering customer engagement and loyalty. It's a strategic move to grow the business and stay competitive in the adventure tourism industry.

Audience

The target audience for the water rafting website is adventure-seeking individuals and groups who are interested in experiencing the thrill of white-water rafting and outdoor enthusiasts looking for memorable and adrenaline-pumping activities. Scenario Questions and Answers: 1. Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on). The target customers primarily include individuals aged 18 to 45, who are in good physical condition and seek adventurous outdoor activities. They are likely to have a moderate to above-average disposable income, as rafting is considered a premium adventure experience. Their interests revolve around outdoor adventures, nature, and adrenaline-pumping activities. These customers may be students, young professionals, or families seeking exciting vacation experiences. 2. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Customers accessing the website expect to find comprehensive information about the rafting experiences offered, including details on different rafting levels (beginner to advanced), trip durations, pricing, and safety measures. They want to know about the scenic locations, the expertise of the guides, and the equipment provided. Additionally, customers will seek information about availability, booking procedures, and any ongoing promotions. They also expect to find customer reviews and testimonials to gauge the quality of the experiences. 3. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? The target audience is likely to access the website through a variety of devices, including desktop computers, laptops, and mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. They may research rafting experiences during work breaks, plan trips from home on laptops, or access the site while on the go using mobile devices. Therefore, it's crucial to ensure that the website is responsive and user-friendly across all these platforms to accommodate their browsing habits and preferences.
